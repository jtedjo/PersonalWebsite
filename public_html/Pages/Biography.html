About Me

Originally from Jakarta, Indonesia, we moved to Southern California in the early 2000s.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Entomology from UC Riverside in 2013 and then a 2nd Bacc in CS from UC Irvine in 2019.

I like to joke and say that I have moved from dealing with real life bugs to virtual ones!

I have experience in various languages, but primarily in Java and C/C++. I also use Git/GitHub, Bash scripting/tools, Linux, Windows for development. My primary interest is in Software Development.

Some of the interesting jobs I've had include working for The Smithsonian Natural History Museum in D.C., Walt Disney World in Florida, CSUSM working on bioinformatics software tools.

I am a great team member with proven track of effective communication both in professional as well as general capacity. I have worked in private and public sector and be part of the company's culture.

On my free time, I enjoy weight-lifting, spending time outdoors watching Netflix, playing ultimate frisbee, and figuring out new tech/tools.
